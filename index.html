Frequently Asked Questions

Collapse all

How is this Story Checklist organised? 

By default, the main story as presented in the in-game timeline of Dragonfable's Book of Lore are visible.
Side Quests are questlines that are not part of the main story, but are visible on the timeline.
Optional Quests are quests that are not visible on the timeline.
Finally, Seasonal quests are content that are generally not available all year round, unless you own the Storybook Collection house item. The large majority of seasonal events are completely disposable: they're often irrelevant, and very self-contained. Skipping seasonal events would generally not hurt your understanding of the story by any means.

Why are some seasonal events so close to each other?

Staff in the past often disregarded chronology when pushing seasonal content. As such, most of them don't have an exact spot to be in the storyline. It is advised to play through them with a high suspension of disbelief, while also ignoring the fact that there's supposed to be a year between chapters.

Do I need to follow the Story Checklist in order? 

Only if you're looking for a better understanding of the story. Otherwise, no.

Why is Book 2 so short?

Staff were undergoing major changes at the time. You can read more about it here.

Some optional quests are missing. Why are they not listed?

If a quest is truly irrelevant, offers no story at all, no character development, doesn't add to anything, and is generally not worth your time, then it won't be included.

Why is Chapter 2 of the Mogloween storybook not listed?

Chapter 2 of the Mogloween storybook was released as The Necropolis.

Can I use this for multiple characters?

Yes. There's a profile feature where you can create, edit, delete, import and export your own profiles. You can find it by clicking the gear icon on the top right.

How are changes saved?

Changes are saved on your browser's cache. Clearing your cache will also clear your saved changes. Alternatively, you can export profiles and save them on your computer.
